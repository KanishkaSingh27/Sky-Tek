About Us

SKY Technology Inc. is Consulting, IT Staff Augmentation & IT Services Company . We work with clients in a variety of verticals and provide multi-platform application solutions.

SKY Technology, Inc. is dedicated to providing the highest quality information technology consulting services and permanent employee placements to our clients as their Business Information Technology Partner. This is achieved through the in-depth use and understanding of today's emerging technologies and the commitment of a dedicated, highly motivated staff. We pride ourselves on delivering to our clients the most value in the Information Technology arena.

SKY Technology Inc. is an offshore IT outsourcing company and was incorporated in 2005 in NJ, USA. In 2006, opened another office in Delhi NCR, India for South Asian clients. The company's founding vision was to specialize in providing its clients cost effective, high quality and commercially viable software services and solutions. From its inception this center has provided IT Staff Augmentation and Project Solutions to our customers in the world wide locations. Services are provided in a timely, cost-effective, and value-added manner. Innovative technology in imaging, data capture, document content capture and collaborative workflow to deliver efficient, fast and error-free back office transactions. We specialize in providing services to Healthcare, Insurance, Banking & Finance, Government and Education sector.
